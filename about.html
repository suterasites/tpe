About us

Two generations. Thirty years in construction. Twenty in screw piling.

Total Piling & Excavations grew out of three decades of foundation and construction work across Victoria. Twenty of those years have been spent specifically on screw piles - long enough to have seen every soil profile, every site condition, and every kind of build the state has to offer. Suburban backyards in Melbourne's east. Government infrastructure. Modular commercial pads. Waterfront residential on Port Phillip. Remote rural builds on the Murray.

Rob runs the operations side, Tom handles sales and customer relationships, and the broader family has worked across the business for years. When you call us, you're getting decisions made by the people whose name is on the truck. No call centres, no escalation chain, no chasing a project manager for an update. That model has held the business together as it has grown from residential work into government projects, and it's what we trade on.

Every pile we drive goes in to the same AS2159 standard whether it's a single residential extension or a multi-pile civil pad. Same engineering rigour, same documentation pack handed over at the end. That's how a small family business stays in front of the bigger names.

Our fleet

The right machine for every site

Modern, regularly serviced machinery from 1.7 tonne mini-excavators through to 8 tonne heavy capacity. Sized to the job, not borrowed from a hire yard.

1.7T mini excavators

Tight-access work. Mini-excavators that fit through standard doorways and side gates for underpinning and rear-yard residential installs.

3T to 5T mid-range

The everyday workhorse fleet. Residential pads, extensions, pool surrounds, small commercial sites - the range that covers most of the work we do.

5T to 8T heavy

Larger commercial pads, multi-house developments, and civil pads. Heavier drive motors, larger pile sizes, programmed grids.

Drive motors & tools

Excavator-mounted drive motors sized across the full standard pile range (76 to 114mm) with custom adapters for larger sizes when projects call for it.

Stocked pile inventory

Large on-hand inventory of 100% Australian-made screw piles by A.A. Steel Works. No waiting on supply for stocked sizes - dispatched direct from our own inventory.

Maintained on schedule

Every machine on a service schedule, every operator ticketed and certified. Reliable kit on site is the difference between a one-day job and a return visit.
