How to reach us

Which line, which inbox.

If you're quoting a project, plans and engineering reports go to sales@totalpiling.com.au. That inbox goes to Rob and Tom direct, and the response comes back with an itemised quote. For anything urgent on a live job, phone is best - Rob and Tom take calls Monday to Friday 7 AM to 5 PM, and Saturday 10 AM to 1 PM.

For screw pile orders (you install the piles yourself), the same sales@ inbox handles supply enquiries - put "Screw pile order" in the subject. Stocked sizes ship from our own inventory in Victoria, with a firm dispatch date confirmed on your quote. Tom handles the sales relationships day to day - 0448 725 807 if you'd rather text or speak directly.

Site visits across Melbourne metro happen most days. Regional Victoria runs through the Echuca base. Interstate work is quoted on request when the project scope justifies the travel - get in touch with the destination and the scope and we'll come back with whether it's a fit.
