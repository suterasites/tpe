Core specialty

Screw Piling

The foundation method we've built our business around. 20 years of certified installs from residential backyards to government sites.

Core specialty

Fast, certified, immediate load.

Steel shafts with helical plates rotated into the ground to load-bearing depth. No excavation pit, no concrete curing, no spoil to truck off site. Most installs are done in hours and accept full structural load the same day. Engineered to AS2159 with a certificate handed over on completion.

  • Hours not days, immediate load capability
  • Residential, commercial, civil, and government sites
  • Tight-access and remote-site capable
  • Works in reactive clay, sand, and most soil types
  • AS2159 certified on completion, certificate provided
Best for New builds, extensions, decks, modular homes, pool surrounds, commercial pads.
Capacity 76mm to 114mm standard, larger sizes by custom order. Residential piles typically rated 2,000 to 10,000+ kg each.
Soil suitability Most soil profiles. Especially strong in reactive clay where traditional concrete piers struggle.

Existing structures

Screw Pile Underpinning

Stabilise settling foundations on developed sites. Minimal excavation, minimal disruption to the home or business.

Tight-access capable

Foundations that have moved, brought back to spec.

If you're seeing cracking walls, uneven floors, sticking doors, or visible settlement on an older home or commercial building, underpinning with screw piles is the cleanest fix available. We work from the perimeter inward through tight-access points, drive piles to load-bearing depth, transfer the existing structural load onto the new piles, and certify the result. Most jobs leave the site looking like nothing happened.

  • Settling foundations, cracking walls, uneven floors
  • Minimal excavation, no large concrete pours
  • Mini-excavator access through doorways and side gates
  • Reactive clay, sloping sites, and infill blocks
  • Engineered, load-certified, AS2159 compliant
Best for Subsiding heritage homes, slab edge lift, post-construction settlement, additions tying into older foundations.
Process Engineering assessment, install plan, drive piles, transfer load, certify and document.
Disruption Low. Most jobs are done in 1 to 3 days without families having to vacate.

Excavation protection

Shoring & Protection

From lightweight residential panels to heavy-duty commercial shoring. Sized to your dig, installed safely, removed cleanly.

Residential to civil

Hold the dig open. Hold the neighbour's fence up.

Any time you cut into the ground next to existing structures, fences, or boundary lines, you need shoring. We supply and install the full range, from lightweight residential systems for pool digs and basement excavations through to heavy commercial panels for civil and infrastructure work. All systems are engineered for the site loads and removed once the permanent structure takes over.

  • Residential pool, basement, and extension excavations
  • Commercial site cuts and infrastructure shoring
  • Retaining wall systems for permanent installations
  • Boundary protection for neighbouring structures
  • Hire or supply-and-install pricing
Best for Pool surrounds, basements, civil excavations, retaining walls, boundary protection.
System types Lightweight residential panels, structural commercial systems, brackets, props, sheet piling for water-table cases.
Engineering Each install is engineered for the actual site loads and signed off before excavation begins.

How we work

From first call to certified install

A clear five-step process that gives you a clear price and a foundation you can build on.

  1. 01

    Send the details

    Email plans, engineering reports, and a soil report through to sales@totalpiling.com.au, or call Rob direct. We quote off real numbers, not guesses.

  2. 02

    Itemised quote

    You get an itemised quote, plain English, no surprises on installation day.

  3. 03

    Engineered to spec

    We confirm pile sizing, depth, and load capacity against your engineer's design and the soil profile. Adjustments before we book in, not after.

  4. 04

    Install on site

    Crew arrives with the right machine and pile stock for the job. Most residential installs are done in a single day. Tight-access sites and large civil pads scheduled accordingly.

  5. 05

    Certified and handed over

    Load testing, AS2159 certification, and full documentation handed over. You build on it the same day.
