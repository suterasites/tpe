Where we've worked

From inner Melbourne to the Murray.

The gallery below is a slice of recent and historic Total Piling work across Victoria. Tier-1 government projects in the Melbourne CBD, residential pads in every suburb of the metro, waterfront homes on Port Phillip, pool surrounds in tight-access backyards, and rural builds reaching from the Bellarine to Echuca and beyond.

Each project is its own engineering problem. The reactive clay under a Mornington Peninsula home is not the same as the loose fill on a Werribee development pad or the rocky profile of a Yarra Valley sloping site. What stays constant is how we approach it - quote off real soil and engineering data inside 24 hours, install to AS2159 with torque logged per pile, certify and hand over the documentation pack the same day the trades arrive.
