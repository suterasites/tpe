Common questions

Ordering, answered

Is there a minimum order quantity?

No. We supply from single-pile orders for small residential extensions through to programmed deliveries for commercial and civil projects. The price per pile changes with quantity, but there is no order minimum.

What sizes do you stock?

We stock the standard residential and light-commercial range: 76mm, 89mm, 102mm, and 114mm shafts with single or double helix plates. Standard lengths from 1.5m through to 6m. Larger shaft sizes and non-standard lengths are produced to order by our manufacturing partner A.A. Steel Works.

How fast can you dispatch?

Stocked sizes are dispatched from our own inventory in Victoria. Custom sizes and non-standard lengths run 1 to 2 weeks depending on production load. We confirm a firm dispatch date on the quote, not a window - so you can plan your install around it.

Do you supply engineering data and certificates with the order?

Yes, always. Every order ships with mill certificates for the steel, engineering data including load tables for the pile sizes supplied, and AS2159 compliance documentation. This is what your engineer and council need to sign off on the install.

Can I pick up from your yard, or do you deliver?

Both. Pickup is available by appointment from our Melbourne and Echuca bases. Delivery across Victoria is quoted on the order based on volume and destination. Interstate delivery available on request - get in touch with the destination postcode for a freight quote.
